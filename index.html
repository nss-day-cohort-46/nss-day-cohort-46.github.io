Thank You!

Many of us came to NSS with the hope of starting over in our careers during the pandemic at a time that shook up our lives. We were faced with the task of diving into something as challenging as learning multiple new languages in such a short period of time. Forced to go beyond our comfort zone and push past the self-doubt on a daily basis, we grew as a class, as a team—-and as software engineers.

Thank you to our extraordinary instructors. We definitely could not have pushed past our comfort levels without your much-needed encouragement, patience, and support. Jisie, Hannah, Scott, Bryan, Aja, Adam, and Jayna are remarkable educators, and we truly cherish the time we have had with you.

We began this journey knowing it would be challenging, but worth it in the end. It brings us so much joy and pride to know that we are capable of learning so much in such a short about of time. Most importantly, we learned how to take our new skillsets and apply them to any new languages, technologies, and challenges we pursue in the future.

Finally, a huge thank you to Nick Carver, Kylie Anyce, Madison Powell, Brittney Potts, Cheo, Stacey Littrell, Erica Thompson and Caleb James and everyone who worked on the website for working together to create the class website—-all while learning server-side technology!
